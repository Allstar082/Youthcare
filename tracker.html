How to Use the Tracker

  1. Select Your Start Date: Locate the current or most recent start date of your period on the Calendar Ring (numbered 1–30). Click on the specific day to set your baseline.
  2. Identify Your Phase: Once selected, the ring will automatically update with color-coded indicators:
    • Red: Indicates your Menstruation Phase.
    • Green: Highlights your High Fertility Window.
    • Purple: Marks the Infertile Phase.
  3. Check Today's Status: Look for the Blue Border on the ring; this highlights today's current date relative to your cycle.
  4. Track Your Progress: The Day -- display on the left will update to show exactly which day of your cycle you are currently on, providing real-time feedback based on your input.
